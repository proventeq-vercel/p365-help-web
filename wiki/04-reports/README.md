# Reports

Reports in Proventeq365 fall into three main groups:

- **Workspace Inventory** — A consolidated overview of both Permissions and ROT data collected from the source.
- **Security & Oversharing** — Detailed information about permissions that have been overshared across documents and folders, plus a dedicated External Users view.
- **Storage Optimisation** — A comprehensive analysis of ROT (Redundant, Obsolete, and Trivial) data within the source. Available as a consolidated summary or as separate reports for Duplicate, Obsolete, and Trivial documents.

## Sections

- [Workspace Inventory](./workspace-inventory/README.md) — Consolidated view of workspace content and scanned data.
- [Security & Oversharing](./security-oversharing/README.md) — Detailed view of sharing and permission-related risks.
- [External Users](./external-users/README.md) — Identify, review, and manage external users with access to your environment.
- [Storage Optimization Summary](./storage-optimization/README.md) — Consolidated ROT view across the environment.
- [Duplicate Documents Report](./duplicate-documents-report/README.md) — Identify identical or very similar files stored in multiple locations.
- [Obsolete Documents Report](./obsolete-documents-report/README.md) — Identify files that are no longer relevant, actively used, or required.
- [Trivial Documents Report](./trivial-documents-report/README.md) — Identify low-value or non-business-critical files.
